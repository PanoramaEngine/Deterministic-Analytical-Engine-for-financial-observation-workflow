# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Panorama Engine open source?

No.

This repository documents the architectural and governance model of Panorama Engine.

The operational system is deployed within controlled enterprise environments.

---

## Is Panorama Engine a trading platform?

No.

Panorama Engine is an analytical infrastructure focused on deterministic decision,
formalization and structural accountability.

It is not a consumer-facing execution platform.

---

## Does Panorama Engine optimize for performance?

Performance is not the primary architectural objective.

The system prioritizes determinism, integrity classification and structural demonstrability.

---

## Can Panorama Engine decisions be audited?

Yes.

Each analytical cycle is formally structured, classified, and reproducible under identical input conditions.

Traceability and integrity states are architectural components of the system.

---

## Is this repository a deployable product?

No.

This repository provides architectural documentation and governance structure.

It does not contain operational pipelines, infrastructure code or deployment configurations.

---

## Who is Panorama Engine designed for?

Panorama Engine is designed for:

- Institutional environments  
- Regulated or supervised contexts  
- Governance and risk oversight structures  
- High-accountability analytical domains  

It is not intended for retail or speculative consumer use.
